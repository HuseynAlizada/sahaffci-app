Our Story

Sahhafçi Bookcafe was born from a simple idea: creating a space that feels just like home. Our founder transformed his apartment into a unique coffee shop where comfort is the priority.

When you enter Sahhafçi, we invite you to take off your shoes - just as you would at home. Surrounded by vintage decor and the warm ambiance of an old money aesthetic, you'll find yourself in a tranquil escape from the outside world.

Every corner of our space is designed to make you feel comfortable, relaxed, and inspired - whether you're here to enjoy our carefully crafted menu, read a book, or simply unwind in good company.
